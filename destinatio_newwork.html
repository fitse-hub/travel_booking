About New York

New York City comprises 5 boroughs sitting where the Hudson River meets the Atlantic Ocean. At its core is Manhattan, a densely populated borough that's among the world's major commercial, financial and cultural centers. Its iconic sites include skyscrapers such as the Empire State Building and sprawling Central Park.

Broadway theater is staged in neon-lit Times Square. The city is the home of the United Nations Headquarters and has been described as the cultural, financial, and media capital of the world. With over 8.8 million residents distributed over 302.6 square miles, New York is also the most densely populated major city in the United States.
