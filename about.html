About TravelEase

Your trusted partner for unforgettable travel experiences since 2010

Our Story

How we became a leading travel platform

TravelEase was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to make travel planning easy, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone. What started as a small startup with just 5 employees has grown into a global travel platform serving millions of travelers worldwide.

Our journey began when our founder, Fitsum Gashaw, experienced the frustrations of planning a complex international trip. She envisioned a platform that would simplify the process and provide travelers with all the tools and information they need in one place.

Over the years, we've expanded our services to include flights, hotels, vacation packages, and destination guides. We've partnered with thousands of airlines, hotels, and local businesses to offer our customers the best possible deals and experiences.

Today, TravelEase is proud to be one of the most trusted names in the travel industry, with offices in 15 countries and a team of over 500 dedicated travel enthusiasts who share a passion for exploration and discovery.

Our Mission

At TravelEase, our mission is to empower people to experience the world by making travel planning simple, accessible, and enjoyable for everyone. We believe that travel has the power to transform lives, broaden perspectives, and create lasting memories.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone can explore and experience different cultures, landscapes, and adventures without the stress and complexity that often comes with travel planning. We strive to be the most trusted and innovative travel platform, continuously improving our services to meet the evolving needs of modern travelers.

Our Values

  • Customer First: We prioritize our customers' needs and satisfaction in everything we do.
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty, transparency, and ethical standards.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek new ways to improve and enhance the travel experience.
  • Diversity: We celebrate and respect the diversity of cultures, perspectives, and experiences.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to promoting responsible and sustainable travel practices.
