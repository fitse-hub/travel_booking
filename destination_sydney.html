About Sydney

Sydney, capital of New South Wales and one of Australia's largest cities, is best known for its harbourfront Sydney Opera House, with a distinctive sail-like design. Massive Darling Harbour and the smaller Circular Quay port are hubs of waterside life, with the arched Harbour Bridge and esteemed Royal Botanic Garden nearby.

Sydney Tower's outdoor platform, the Skywalk, offers 360-degree views of the city and suburbs. Sydney's beaches, including popular Bondi and Manly, offer fantastic swimming, surfing, and coastal walks. The city is also home to the Royal National Park, the world's second-oldest national park.
